Criteria for music

The objectives of MODE concerning the presentation of the musical text of Marenzio’s secular works are: a) to provide its users with a text derived from a systematic collation of the sources, including the surviving copies of the first edition, and b) to display the musical text in a form that facilitates the study and the performance of the music.

Criteria for poetic text

The goal of MODE concerning the presentation of the poetic text of Marenzio’s secular works is to provide performers and scholars with a correct, accurate, and readable text. The editorial criteria aim to maintain many of their original features, but to adapt others to the rules of modern Italian, for example spelling, accents and apostrophes.
